Rules of Blackjack Dice!

Blackjack Dice is the dice version of the popular card game Blackjack!
  • In each turn players will click either the "PLAYER 1 CLICK TO ROLL" or the "PLAYER 2 CLICK TO ROLL" buttons in order to start their turn. This button will roll two dice and the sum will be added to their turn total. Players can roll as many times as they wish.
  • Once players have scored 16 or more, they have the option of switching to rolling only one dice.
  • If a players score goes above 21, they have busted and the other player wins the round.
  • When a player wins a round, 2 points are added to their final score
  • The goal of the game is to score 21 or get closer to 21 than your oponent without going over.
  • If none of the players score 21, then the player with the closest score will get the points.
  • This game goes on for 10 rounds and the first player to win 10, will win the game.
